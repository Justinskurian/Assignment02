About Countryside Library


Welcome to Countryside Library

At Countryside Library, we believe in the transformative power of reading and community engagement. Nestled in the heart of our town, our library has been a cornerstone of knowledge, creativity, and connection for over 50 years. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone—regardless of age, background, or interests—can discover the joy of learning and the value of community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect people through the love of books and the exchange of ideas. We are committed to providing access to diverse resources and fostering a lifelong love of reading in a supportive and inclusive space.

Our History

Founded in 1973, Countryside Library started as a small community initiative with just a few shelves of donated books. Over the years, we have grown into a comprehensive resource center with thousands of books, multimedia collections, and digital resources. Our library has evolved with the times, but our commitment to serving the community has remained steadfast.
