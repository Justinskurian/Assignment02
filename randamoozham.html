Randamoozham



Author : M T Vasudevan Nair

Experience the epic tale of the Mahabharata from the unique perspective of Bhima, the often overshadowed second Pandava. "Randamoozham" offers a powerful retelling of this legendary story, delving deep into the emotions, struggles, and inner conflicts of one of its most complex characters. M.T. Vasudevan Nair's masterful narrative brings to life the human side of the epic, challenging traditional views and offering a fresh, compelling take. This novel is not just a retelling, but a profound exploration of destiny, duty, and the price of greatness.
