AADUJEEVITHAM



Author : Benyamin

Follow the harrowing journey of Najeeb, a migrant worker trapped in the deserts of Saudi Arabia. "Aadujeevitham" is a powerful tale of survival, resilience, and the human spirit's unyielding hope against unimaginable odds. Benyamin's gripping narrative sheds light on the harsh realities faced by many migrant laborers, making this novel both a compelling and thought-provoking read. This story is a testament to the endurance of the human will, reminding us of the deep bonds that sustain us through life's most difficult trials.
